Personal OSINT Framework

Phase 2: List of data types

In a personal OSINT investigation, we may want to obtain many types of data. Here we have some examples.

  • Full name and surname
  • Aliases or usernames on social networks
  • Email addresses
  • Passwords (if publicly available)
  • Telephone numbers
  • Call or message logs (if publicly available)
  • Residential addresses
  • Previous residential addresses
  • Geolocation data (routes and frequent zones)
  • Workplace and position
  • Previous workplaces and positions
  • Spoken languages
  • Education and certifications
  • Attended colleges and universities
  • Graduation dates
  • Important dates such as birthdays and anniversaries
  • Social media details (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn, etc.)
  • Social media posts and their content
  • Frequency of social media activity
  • Personal photos and videos
  • Comments on forums and online discussions
  • Online opinions and reviews
  • Websites where activity has taken place
  • Subscriptions to podcasts or video channels
  • Media consumption patterns (TV shows, movies)
  • Memberships in groups or associations
  • Participation in events or conferences
  • Participation in charity events or volunteer activities
  • Donations to causes or campaigns
  • Use of public or community services
  • Public transport preferences
  • Diet and dietary restrictions
  • Known allergies
  • Lifestyle information and habits
  • Hobbies and leisure activities
  • Main interests
  • Travel history
  • Recently visited events and places
  • Future travel plans
  • Subscriptions to magazines or services
  • Memberships in clubs or gyms
  • Memberships in unions or professional guilds
  • Favorite sports and sports teams
  • Participation in sports competitions or events
  • Online gaming accounts and related activity
  • Family information
  • Information about romantic partners
  • Information about friends
  • Pets and pet names
  • Legal or judicial records
  • Vehicle ownership records
  • Real estate ownership records
  • Marriage and divorce records
  • Registered patents or copyrights
  • Official documents
  • Traffic violations
  • Financial health
  • Credit and financial statements
  • Debts and loans
  • Tax and insurance information
  • Recent online purchases
  • Stock or cryptocurrency investments
  • Vehicle plate numbers
  • Political affiliations
  • Religious beliefs
  • Intellectual property, including trademarks
  • Academic publications or written articles
  • Awards or recognitions received
  • Attendance at VIP or exclusive events
  • Interactions with influencers or public figures
  • Public health information
  • Emergency information or incidents
  • Online wish lists or gift registries
  • Software or hardware preferences
  • Device security and network configurations
  • Professional licenses
